Banjo River Run

Future Plans

None at this point. I have more ideas for the game but I'm far more interested in other projects at the moment and feel content with the current state of the game. That's life man.

However, if I did work on it again, more boss fights would definitely be in order. A whole bevy of different obstacle types would also be introduced (rocks, animals), with complimetary new movement abilities like "dodge" (double tap a side to quickly move that way). I'd also try to work on the progression system to reward good players with less work during the easier levels, allowing them to reach an appropriate challenge for their skills faster during each run.
